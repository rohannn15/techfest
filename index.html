About TechMantra

TechMantra is our college's annual technical fest that celebrates innovation, creativity, and engineering excellence.

It is a platform where students showcase their skills in technology, collaborate in teams, and participate in thrilling competitions and workshops.

Events and Games

Here are some of the exciting activities we conduct during TechMantra:

  • Hackathon – Coding competition
  • Chess Tournament – For strategy and focus
  • Robotics Challenge – Build and control robots
  • Paper Presentation – Showcase your innovative ideas
  • Tech Quiz – Test your knowledge of technology and trends
  • Poster Design – Express tech creatively

Each event is designed to challenge your skills, encourage teamwork, and provide a platform for learning and growth.
